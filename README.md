# 🎧 SupportPilot AI

Transforming Customer Interactions Into Automated Service Excellence

---

## Overview

SupportPilot AI is an enterprise-grade customer support automation platform designed to streamline customer service operations through Conversational AI, Agentic Workflows, Knowledge Retrieval, and Intelligent Automation.

Modern support organizations handle thousands of customer inquiries daily across multiple channels. SupportPilot AI enables businesses to automate repetitive support tasks, improve response quality, accelerate issue resolution, and deliver consistent customer experiences at scale.

By combining Large Language Models (LLMs), Agentic AI, Conversational Memory, Knowledge Retrieval, and Workflow Automation, the platform serves as an intelligent support assistant capable of understanding customer intent, retrieving relevant information, and executing business actions.
